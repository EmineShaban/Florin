What is Total Eye Lift?

Total Eye Lift is a versatile eye serum, which is as effective as retinol**. It can help to reduce puffy under eyes, improve the appearance of dark circles, and smooth crow’s feet.

This silky cream-gel glides onto the eye area to lift, smooth and brighten from temple to temple.

This serum is derived from a variety of natural plant extracts, including Organic Harungana - nature’s retinol. As effective as retinol, harungana stimulates the production of collagen in the skin to help fight against wrinkles – but without any dryness or irritation. It is well tolerated by all skin types, even sensitive ones.

Horse chestnut extract helps to brighten dark, shadowy areas, whilst the organic Albizia extract reduces sagging under eye skin, restoring radiance immediately.

Exclusive Application Methods

Store your Total Eye Lift Serum in the fridge to reduce redness and puffiness in the morning. To apply, dab a small amount gently onto the eye contour and eyelids. Smooth gently between the eyebrows, on the temples and all around the eye area.

Warm the product

Bring a small amount of product to skin temperature by warming it between your fingers, so that your skin accepts it instantly.

3 seconds

Lymphatic Drainage

Press gently with the pads of your fingers, starting at the inside corners of eyes, working outward toward temples. Repeat 4 times below and above the eyes, following the natural direction of lymphatic circulation. Then, apply relaxing pressure between the brows.

6 seconds

FAQS

What is Florin Total Eye Lift?

Florin Total Eye Lift is an advanced eye care product designed to target all signs of aging around the eye area. It helps lift and firm the skin, reduce the appearance of wrinkles, fine lines, puffiness, and dark circles, and enhance the overall radiance of the eye contour.

Who is Total Eye Lift suitable for?

Total Eye Lift is suitable for all skin types and ages. It's perfect for anyone looking to address common eye area concerns, such as loss of firmness, wrinkles, and dark circles.

How do I use Florin Total Eye Lift?

Apply morning and/or evening after cleansing. Gently tap a small amount of the product around the eye contour area, from the inner corner of the eye outwards, and smooth gently. For best results, use with other Florin skincare products.

Can I use Total Eye Lift with makeup?

Yes, Total Eye Lift absorbs quickly into the skin, making it an ideal base for makeup application. It helps smooth the skin's texture, allowing for easier application of concealer and other makeup products.

How long does it take to see results?

Results may vary, but many users notice an immediate lifting effect after application, with visible improvements in firmness, smoothness, and reduction of dark circles within a few weeks of consistent use.
