FAQS

What is Clarins Super Restorative Remodelling Serum?

The Clarins Super Restorative Remodelling Serum is an anti-aging serum designed to target skin slackening and age spots. It aims to visibly lift, reshape, and redefine facial contours while diminishing the appearance of deep wrinkles and dark spots.

Who is this serum suitable for?

This serum is suitable for all skin types and is specifically designed for women experiencing age-related hormonal changes that impact skin appearance, typically from age 50 and above.

How do I use the Super Restorative Remodelling Serum?

Apply the serum morning and/or evening to clean face and neck. Use gentle pressing motions, working from the center of the face outward.

What are the main ingredients in this serum?

Key ingredients include organic harungana extract to help restore density, Montpellier rock-rose extract to reduce the appearance of age spots, and plant sugars to improve skin tone and firmness.
